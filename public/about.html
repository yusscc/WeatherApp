About WeatherDash

Learn more about our weather dashboard application

Our Mission

WeatherDash is designed to provide accurate, up-to-date weather information in a clean, intuitive interface. Our goal is to help you plan your day, week, or even your travel with reliable weather forecasts from around the world.

Features

Global Coverage

Access weather data for cities worldwide with our interactive map.

Customization

Personalize your dashboard with themes and display preferences.

Detailed Forecasts

View hourly and 7-day forecasts with comprehensive weather data.

Technology

WeatherDash uses reliable weather data from trusted meteorological sources. This application is built with modern web technologies including HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and Leaflet.js for interactive maps.
